COVID-19 Updates

Due to the ongoing coronavirus pandemic, we here at Hodgdon Food-on-the-run are complying with all new university procedures and policies to keep students and staff safe. Our new procedures are as follows:

  • All food from hodgdon is now mobile-order-only for takeout
  • There are new traffic flow procedures and guidelines for people shopping in Hodgdon Food-on-the-run. These are marked on the floor of the store
  • All staff working are required to wear masks
  • All students shopping are required to wear masks
  • Hodgdon Food-on-the-run now has a maximum occupancy of 7 people (not including workers)
  • Social distancing markers have been placed in front of all stations where lines form
  • The store is disinfected 3 times a day by cleaning staff
  • Shoppers now swipe their own cards to avoid exposing staff to student ID cards or credit cards
  • Previously self-serve soup and pastries are now pre-packaged

We appreciate your patience and commitment to keeping our community safe!
